# Brand Guidelines - Examples

## Example 1: Developer Tool (B2B SaaS)

### Brand Personality
**Core Values:**
- Transparency over black boxes
- Speed over perfection
- Developers first, always

**Personality Traits:**
- **Technical but approachable:** We explain complex concepts clearly
- **Direct:** No marketing fluff, just facts
- **Pragmatic:** Ship working code, iterate later

**Archetype:** The Sage (provides knowledge and insights)

### Voice & Tone
**Voice:** Technical, honest, helpful

**Tone Examples:**
- **Onboarding:** "5-minute setup. Copy, paste, ship."
- **Error:** "Deploy failed. Line 42: syntax error in config.yml"
- **Success:** "Deployed to production in 2.3s"

### Visual Direction
**Colors:**
- Primary: Dark slate (`#1a1f2e`) - terminal vibes
- Accent: Neon green (`#00ff88`) - command line success
- Functional: Monochrome grays

**Typography:**
- Primary: JetBrains Mono (monospace)
- UI: Inter (clean sans-serif)

**Style:** Minimal, high contrast, code-inspired

## Example 2: Consumer Wellness App (B2C)

### Brand Personality
**Core Values:**
- Progress over perfection
- Self-compassion over self-criticism
- Science-backed, not pseudo-science

**Personality Traits:**
- **Warm but not saccharine:** Encouraging without being condescending
- **Evidence-based:** Cite research, not trends
- **Human:** Acknowledge that wellness is hard

**Archetype:** The Caregiver (nurtures and supports)

### Voice & Tone
**Voice:** Warm, evidence-based, non-judgmental

**Tone Examples:**
- **Onboarding:** "Welcome! Let's find what works for you."
- **Missed goal:** "That's okay. Tomorrow's a new day."
- **Achievement:** "3 days in a row! You're building a habit."

### Visual Direction
**Colors:**
- Primary: Soft sage green (`#a8c5b0`) - calm, natural
- Accent: Warm coral (`#ff8b7c`) - energy without aggression
- Neutrals: Warm beiges and creams

**Typography:**
- Primary: Epilogue (friendly, rounded sans-serif)
- Accent: Crimson Text (serif for warmth)

**Style:** Soft, rounded corners (12px), generous whitespace, nature photography

## Example 3: Financial Planning Tool (B2B/B2C)

### Brand Personality
**Core Values:**
- Clarity over complexity
- Empowerment over gatekeeping
- Real wealth over performative wealth

**Personality Traits:**
- **Straightforward:** No financial jargon without explanation
- **Empowering:** You can do this, we'll help
- **Realistic:** Acknowledge financial anxiety is normal

**Archetype:** The Sage + The Caregiver (wise guide who supports)

### Voice & Tone
**Voice:** Clear, confident, empathetic

**Tone Examples:**
- **Onboarding:** "Let's build your financial plan. No judgment, just progress."
- **Bad news:** "Your spending is 20% over budget. Here's how to adjust."
- **Good news:** "You're on track to save $5,000 this year."

### Visual Direction
**Colors:**
- Primary: Deep blue (`#1e3a5f`) - trust, stability
- Accent: Optimistic gold (`#f4c430`) - growth, aspiration
- Functional: Standard (green = good, red = attention)

**Typography:**
- Primary: Manrope (modern, professional sans-serif)
- Numbers: IBM Plex Mono (clarity for data)

**Style:** Clean, data-focused, charts and graphs with clear labels

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

### The "Everything Company"
**Mistake:**
- Values: "Innovation, Quality, Customer Focus, Integrity, Teamwork"
- Could describe literally any company

**Fix:**
- Be specific: "We ship half-baked features and iterate based on usage, not perfection"

### The Personality Crisis
**Mistake:**
- Copy: "Yo! Check out our sick new features! ðŸ”¥"
- Visuals: Corporate blue, formal serif fonts

**Fix:**
- Match voice and visuals. If playful, be playful everywhere.

### The Thesaurus Attack
**Mistake:**
- "Leverage our innovative paradigm-shifting solution to synergize your workflow"

**Fix:**
- "Get your work done faster"

### The Generic Tech Startup
**Mistake:**
- Blue/purple gradient
- Sans-serif font
- "We're making [industry] simple"

**Fix:**
- Find ONE thing that's actually different and emphasize it
