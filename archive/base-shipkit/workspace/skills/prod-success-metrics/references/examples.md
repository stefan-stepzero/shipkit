| ‚Üí Paying customers | 42/mo | 180/mo | 20% ‚Üí 30% |

**Key Results:**
- KR1: Increase activation rate from 35% to 50%

| Conversion Rate | 1.2% | 1.6% | üî¥ |

## Example 4: Developer Tool - API Monitoring SaaS (MVP Stage)

### North Star Metric
**APIs Monitored by Paying Customers**
- Current: 420 APIs
- Target: 1,500 APIs (by Q3)
- Definition: Unique API endpoints under active paid monitoring

**Why:** More APIs monitored = more value delivered = higher retention and expansion.

### Primary Metrics

| Metric | Current | Target | Status |
|--------|---------|--------|--------|
| Free Signups | 180/mo | 300/mo | üü° |
| Free ‚Üí Paid Conversion | 12% | 20% | üî¥ |
| MRR | $8,400 | $25,000 | üü° |
| Logo Churn (monthly) | 4% | <3% | üî¥ |
| Net Dollar Retention | 95% | >100% | üî¥ |

### Activation & Engagement

| Metric | Current | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Time to First Alert | 8 minutes | <5 min |
| % users who add 2nd API within week 1 | 45% | 60% |
| % users who integrate Slack/PagerDuty | 35% | 55% |
| Alerts sent per user per week | 3.2 | 4+ |
| False positive rate | 12% | <8% ‚ö†Ô∏è |

**Issue:** False positive rate too high (12%). Users snooze/ignore alerts ‚Üí churn.

### Pricing Tiers & Expansion

| Tier | # Customers | ARPU | Churn |
|------|-------------|------|-------|
| Free | 1,200 | $0 | 15%/mo |
| Starter ($49/mo, 5 APIs) | 42 | $49 | 6%/mo |
| Pro ($149/mo, 20 APIs) | 28 | $149 | 3%/mo |
| Enterprise ($499/mo, unlimited) | 8 | $650 (avg $499-$999) | 0%/mo |

**Insight:** Higher tiers have much lower churn. Focus on upselling Starter ‚Üí Pro.

### Expansion Metrics

| Metric | Current | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| % customers who upgrade tier within 3 months | 18% | 30% |
| % customers who add team members | 22% | 40% |
| Expansion MRR (from upsells) | $840/mo | $2,500/mo |
| Contraction MRR (from downgrades) | $420/mo | <$500/mo |
| Net Expansion Rate | +5% | +15% |

**Calculate Net Dollar Retention:**
```
NDR = (Starting MRR + Expansion - Churn - Contraction) / Starting MRR
NDR = ($8,000 + $840 - $320 - $420) / $8,000 = 102.5%
```

Target: >110% (expansion exceeds churn significantly).

### Product Usage Patterns

**Power users** (high retention, likely to expand):
- Monitor 10+ APIs
- Integrate with Slack or PagerDuty
- Custom alert thresholds set
- Check dashboard 3+ times/week

**At-risk users** (likely to churn):
- Monitor 1-2 APIs only
- No integrations
- Don't check dashboard (rely on emails only)
- High false positive rate (>15%)

**Action:** Identify at-risk users, reach out proactively to help them configure properly.

### OKR for This Quarter

**Objective:** Improve free-to-paid conversion and reduce churn

**Key Results:**
- KR1: Increase free ‚Üí paid conversion from 12% to 20%
- KR2: Reduce logo churn from 4% to <3%
- KR3: Achieve Net Dollar Retention >110% (through expansion)

## Example 5: Marketplace - Freelance Platform (Established Stage)

### North Star Metric
**Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) Per Month**
- Current: $1.2M
- Target: $2M (by end of year)
- Definition: Total value of transactions on platform

**Why:** GMV drives revenue (we take 15% commission), and indicates marketplace health.

### Two-Sided Marketplace Metrics

**Supply Side (Freelancers):**

| Metric | Current | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Active freelancers | 2,400 | 4,000 |
| New freelancer signups/mo | 320 | 500 |
| Freelancer activation rate | 45% (get first gig) | 60% |
| Avg earnings per active freelancer/mo | $850 | $1,000 |
| Freelancer retention (6 months) | 65% | 75% |

**Demand Side (Clients):**

| Metric | Current | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Active clients | 580 | 1,000 |
| New client signups/mo | 95 | 150 |
| Client activation rate | 70% (post first job) | 80% |
| Avg spend per client/mo | $2,200 | $2,500 |
| Client retention (6 months) | 72% | 80% |

### Marketplace Balance

**Liquidity Metric:**
```
Liquidity = (Jobs filled within 48 hours) / (Total jobs posted)
Current: 78%
Target: >85%
```

**Supply/Demand Ratio:**
```
Ratio = Active freelancers / Active clients
Current: 4.1:1 (healthy range: 3:1 to 6:1)
```

**If ratio too high:** Too many freelancers, not enough work ‚Üí freelancers churn
**If ratio too low:** Not enough freelancers, jobs go unfilled ‚Üí clients churn

## Stage-Specific Metric Priorities

### POC Stage
**Focus:** Validation

**Primary metrics:**
1. Problem validation: % of interviews confirming pain
2. Solution validation: % of users completing core workflow
3. Willingness to pay: % who'd pay $X

**Don't track:**
- Revenue (too early)
- Churn (sample size too small)
- Scalability (premature)

### MVP Stage
**Focus:** Product-market fit

**Primary metrics:**
1. North Star metric (value delivered)
2. Retention (Day 7, Day 30, cohort curves)
3. Engagement (DAU/MAU, core action frequency)
4. NPS (user satisfaction)
5. Activation rate (time to value)

**Secondary:**
- CAC (but don't over-optimize yet)
- Early revenue (validate pricing)

**Decision point:** If retention <40% at Day 30 or NPS <30, you don't have PMF yet.

### Established Stage
**Focus:** Sustainable growth

**Track everything:**
1. Growth (MRR, GMV, user growth)
2. Unit economics (LTV, CAC, LTV:CAC, payback)
3. Retention (cohorts, churn, NDR)
4. Engagement (DAU/MAU, feature adoption)
5. Revenue efficiency (CAC payback, gross margin)
6. Market position (market share, NPS vs competitors)

**Optimize for:**
- Profitability
- Predictable growth
- Competitive moat

## Quick Reference: What to Track When

**Week 1 (POC):**
- User interviews conducted
- % confirming problem
- % interested in solution

**Month 1 (MVP):**
- Signups
- Activation rate
- Day 1, Day 7 retention
- Core action frequency

**Month 3 (MVP):**
- All of the above +
- Day 30 retention
- NPS
- Churn rate
- CAC (rough estimate)

**Month 6 (Scaling):**
- All of the above +
- MRR / Revenue
- LTV
- LTV:CAC ratio
- Cohort retention curves
- Feature adoption

**Year 1+ (Established):**
- Full metrics suite
- OKRs aligned to business goals
- Competitive benchmarking
